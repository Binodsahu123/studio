# **App Name**: WriteBot AI

## Core Features:

- AI Content Generation: Leverage GPT-3.5, GPT-4, GPT-4 Turbo, GPT-4o, and GPT-4o Mini to generate high-quality blog posts, marketing copy, and more using a tool to enhance and expand information as needed.
- AI Image Generation: Create stunning visuals using Stable Diffusion and DALL-E, supporting text-to-image, multi-prompting, image-to-image, and image upscaling features.
- Template Library: Access over 70 ready-made templates for various content needs, including blog posts, emails, social media content, and website copy.
- AI ChatBot Support: Get instant support and answers to your questions with our integrated AI ChatBot, available 24/7.
- SEO Optimization: Ensure your content ranks higher with built-in SEO optimization tools that analyze and suggest improvements.
- Plagiarism & AI Content Detection: Verify the originality of your content with our AI Plagiarism Checker and AI Content Detector.
- Multi-Language Support: Generate content in unlimited languages, expanding your reach to a global audience.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Purple (#673AB7) to convey innovation and sophistication.
- Background color: Very Light Grey (#F5F5F5) for a clean, modern look in light mode.
- Accent color: Teal (#009688) for CTAs and interactive elements to stand out against the purple.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a modern and readable experience.
- Use consistent, minimalistic icons across the platform to enhance user navigation and understanding.
- Maintain a clean, intuitive layout with clear information hierarchy to guide users through the content generation process.
- Implement subtle, purposeful animations to provide feedback and enhance user engagement without being distracting.
